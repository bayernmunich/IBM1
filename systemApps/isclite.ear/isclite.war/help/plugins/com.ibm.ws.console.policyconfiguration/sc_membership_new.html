Define service policy memberships

You can define the association of a service policy with a transaction class. Using the combination of a service policy with associated transaction and work classes, the on demand router assesses all work in the queue at any time, and makes decisions based upon the attributes in the service policy.

To view this administrative console page, click Operational policies > Service policies > New. After defining the service policy general properties, click Next to access the Define service policy membership page.

To create, modify, or remove service policies and transaction classes, you must have administrator or configurator administrative privileges.

Members of service_policy_name

This list indicates the names of the transaction classes that are affiliated with your newly defined service policy. The list contains the default transaction class, default_TC. To add transaction classes to the service policy, click New and create your selections from the following panel.

A transaction class can belong to one service policy only.

Click Next when you complete this panel.
