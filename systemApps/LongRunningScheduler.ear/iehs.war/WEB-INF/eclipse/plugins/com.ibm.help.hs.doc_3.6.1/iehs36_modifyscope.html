Modifying the existing scope

You can modify your original scope to meet your new requirement.

Procedure

  1. Click Scopes: on the toolbar.
  2. In the pop-up window choose the targeted scope from the scope list and click Edit.
  3. In the pop-up window
    1. Rename your scope by specifying a new name in the List name field.
    2. Click () to expand the options, and select your scope criteria and topic sets.
  4. Click OK to save your modifications.

What to do next

To use your new modified scope, see Using the existing scope
