Using the existing scope

You can use the existing scope to narrow down your working scope.

Procedure

  1. Click Scope: on the toolbar.
  2. In the pop-up window
    1. Click Show all topics to set the scope of the whole document.
    2. Click Show only the following topics and choose one scope from the scope list.
  3. Click OK to make your working scope effective.
