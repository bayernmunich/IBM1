WebSphere Intelligent Management Pack (IMP)

Public MBean Interfaces

This documentation provides information on the public APIs presented by the MBean that are part of the management system of WebSphere Intelligent Management Pack (IMP). These MBean are accessible programmatically and through the wsadmin command-line scripting tool. Click on an MBean Type at the left for detailed information about that MBean.
